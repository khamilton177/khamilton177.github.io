Do What You Love and You'll Never Work

Bathroom Remodel

The ability to visualize from conception to production is in my DNA. My process when coding, decorating, and cooking is always to determine what outcome I want, implement continuity across different areas, reuse what's good, and add a refreshing or surprise element.

At the time, I didn't have access to any drawing software so I painstakingly created the diagram images in MS Paint (with a mouse!) for my contractor to use.

I researched and selected all the tile, fixtures, and cabinetry types and sizes myself. If I had possessed the know how to install, I would have bypassed using a contractor but, alas, knowing when to engage help is another valuable skill in itself.
